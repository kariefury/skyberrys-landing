Btaii — Privacy Policy

Btaii does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All data — item profiles, repair logs, and photos — is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

Camera

The app requests Camera permission to take photos for item profiles and repair log entries. Photos are stored only on your device and are never uploaded or transmitted.

On-Device Storage

All item records, repair histories, and photos are stored locally in a database on your device. Nothing is backed up to or synced with any external server or cloud service.

QR Code Handover

When you generate a give-away flyer, the item's repair history is encoded directly into the QR code itself as a self-contained data payload. No data is sent to any server during this process — the QR code is generated entirely on-device and the record travels solely within the code. When a new owner scans the code, the data transfers directly from the QR code into their app with no server involved at any point.

Third Parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data of any kind.
