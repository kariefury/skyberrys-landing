How Pranked My House — Privacy Policy

How Pranked My House does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All processing happens entirely on your device and nothing is ever sent to any server.

Camera

The app requests Camera permission to perform real-time object detection on the live camera feed. Frames are processed locally on-device using on-device machine learning models. No images or video are saved, uploaded, or transmitted — the camera feed is used only in the moment and discarded immediately.

On-Device Processing

Object detection and image labelling are performed entirely on-device using Google ML Kit. No data leaves your phone at any point during a scan.

Third Parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data of any kind. Google ML Kit is used solely for on-device inference; no data is sent to Google or any other party through its use.

Children

How Pranked My House is designed to be used by families, including children. In keeping with that, the app collects absolutely no data from any user, of any age.
