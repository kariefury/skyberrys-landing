Orchid Journal — Privacy Policy

Orchid Journal does not collect, transmit, or store any personal information. All data — orchid profiles, daily logs, and photos — is stored only on your device and is never sent to any server.

Permissions

The app requests Location permission solely to satisfy Android's requirement for Bluetooth device discovery, which is used for the optional phone-to-phone orchid transfer feature. Your location is never determined, logged, or shared.

Third Parties

The app contains no advertising, no analytics, and no third-party SDKs that collect data of any kind.
